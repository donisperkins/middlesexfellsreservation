Middlesex Fells Reservation

Your complete guide to hiking, trails, and exploring the Fells near Boston.

About Middlesex Fells

Middlesex Fells Reservation is a 2,500-acre state park just north of Boston, spanning the towns of Medford, Malden, Stoneham, Winchester, and Melrose. It's one of the oldest state parks in Massachusetts and a favorite escape for hikers, trail runners, and families.

Trails

The Fells offers over 100 miles of trails ranging from easy walks to rugged rocky terrain. Popular routes include the Skyline Trail, the Rock Circuit Trail, and the Cross Fells Trail.

Parking & Access

Multiple parking areas are available throughout the reservation. The main entrance is off Fellsway West in Medford. Parking is free.
